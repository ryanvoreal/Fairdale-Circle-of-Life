Food Drive for the Homeless

Our school is currently leading a community-wide food drive to support people experiencing homelessness. Each Crew is responsible for collecting a specific category of food items, promoting the drive, and finding creative ways to bring in donations — even if students cannot donate directly.

What We’re Collecting: Non-perishable items such as canned goods, rice, pasta, boxed meals, peanut butter, and other long-lasting foods.

Timeline:

  • Nov 25 — Crew Planning Day: Crews create collection plans and brainstorm ways to spread awareness.
  • Dec 1 — Collection Begins: Students start bringing in food items. Crew Leaders gather donations. Reach out to family, friends, and community groups.
  • Dec 2–3 — Poster Competition: Crews design posters to advertise the food drive online or in school.
  • Dec 12 — Final Collection & Delivery: STLP students collect all items and deliver them to the Youth Service Center.

Impact Goal: Provide meals for families and individuals in need while building community leadership among students.
