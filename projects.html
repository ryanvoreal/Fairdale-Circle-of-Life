Last Project

Food Drive for the Homeless

Complete • Nov 25 – Dec 12

We are hosting a school-wide food drive to support members of our homeless community. Each Crew is assigned a food category to collect and is encouraged to promote the drive through posters, social outreach, and community engagement.

We're Collecting: Non-perishable foods such as canned goods, dry rice, pasta, peanut butter, and boxed meals.

Timeline

Nov 25 — Crew Planning Day

Crews create their plan for collecting their assigned food items.

Dec 1 — Collection Begins

Students begin bringing in items; Crew Leaders collect donations daily.

Dec 2–3 — Poster Competition

Crews design posters to advertise the drive on campus and social media.

Dec 12 — Final Collection Day

STLP students gather all items and deliver them to the Youth Service Center.

Impact Goal: Provide meals for families in need while building student leadership.

Progress: 100%
Project Results

Food Drive for the Homeless

Complete • Final Impact

Thanks to our students, staff, and community partners, the food drive exceeded its goals and directly supported local families in need.

What We Achieved

  • 560+ non‑perishable items collected
    • 270+ Canned Goods (Beans, Tomatoes, etc.)
    • 120+ Rice/Grains (Rice, Grains, etc.)
    • 90+ Shelf Stable Protein (PB, Jelly, etc.)
    • 60+ Cereal (Froot Loops, Lucky Charms, etc.)
  • 40+ families supported
  • 60+ classes participated

Lasting Impact: Students practiced leadership, empathy, and community organizing while addressing food insecurity.
