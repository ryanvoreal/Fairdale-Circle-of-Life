Food Drive for the Homeless

Complete • Nov 25 – Dec 12

We hosted a school-wide food drive to support members of our homeless community. Each Crew was assigned a food category to collect and promoted the drive through posters, social outreach, and community engagement.

We collected: Non-perishable foods such as canned goods, dry rice, pasta, peanut butter, and boxed meals.

Impact goal: Provide meals for families in need while building student leadership.

Progress: 100%
Nov 25 — Crew Planning Day

Crews created their plan for collecting their assigned food items.

Dec 1 — Collection Begins

Students began bringing in items; Crew Leaders collected donations daily.

Dec 2–3 — Poster Competition

Crews designed posters to advertise the drive on campus and social media.

Dec 12 — Final Collection Day

STLP students gathered all items and delivered them to the Youth Service Center.

Thanks to our students, staff, and community partners, the food drive exceeded its goals and directly supported local families in need.

What We Achieved

  • 560+ non‑perishable items collected
    • 270+ Canned Goods (Beans, Tomatoes, etc.)
    • 120+ Rice/Grains (Rice, Grains, etc.)
    • 90+ Shelf Stable Protein (PB, Jelly, etc.)
    • 60+ Cereal (Froot Loops, Lucky Charms, etc.)
  • 40+ families supported
  • 60+ classes participated

Lasting Impact: Students practiced leadership, empathy, and community organizing while addressing food insecurity.
